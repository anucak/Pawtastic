About us

- Our story -

Well-groomed pets are happier, better behaved, healthier, and more outgoing. Just like all of us, they feel good when they look good. Pawtastic Dog Salon & Wash offers a complete line of fine grooming services to bring out the best of appearance and personality in any breed. We work carefully, never rush, and approach every pet individually. We highly advocate for animal rights and sustainability so the only products that we use are vegan and cruelty-free. Feel free to contact us if you want to pamper your pup with a relaxing spa-like experience!
